— Our Story

A studio built around quiet confidence

Aurelie began in 2014 as a two-chair studio with a simple idea: beauty care should feel like a ritual, not a transaction. A decade later, we're a full-service salon trusted by thousands of clients — still guided by the same attention to detail, unhurried pace, and honest recommendations.

  • Individual consultation before every service
  • Only professional-grade, cruelty-free products
  • Continual training on modern techniques
